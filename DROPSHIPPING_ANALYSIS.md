## üö´ **WHY THIS DOESN'T WORK IN PRACTICE:**

### Technical Issues:
1. **No Walmart Purchase API** - You can't automate buying from Walmart
2. **Inventory Sync Problems** - Stock levels change rapidly
3. **Price Fluctuations** - Walmart prices change frequently
4. **Shipping Delays** - Customer expects Amazon speed
5. **Return Complexity** - Two-platform return process

### Business Risks:
1. **Account Bans** (Amazon sellers lose accounts daily for this)
2. **Negative Reviews** (slow shipping, wrong packaging)  
3. **Margin Compression** (fees eat into profits)
4. **Customer Service Issues** (managing two platforms)
